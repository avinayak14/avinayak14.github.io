Somehow we didn't have a single peach the entire trip, but restaurant-maxxing coincided with state-maxxing. Cafe Intermezzo, Antico, Superica, JenChan's, Sweet Hut, Desi Tadka, Haraz Coffee House, and Yonutz Donuts all in less than 40 hours total. The number of hours awake in Atlanta was around 18, so we hit a restaurant almost every 2 hours awake. We also made it to Krog Street Market, Cabbagetown for the murals, the NCR office for the obligatory tour, the Coca-Cola museum (with free tastings), and the Benz stadium. May this travel momentum persist in 2026 for the 9 holdout states.
